THE POWER PROJECT

After the dark tragedy that caused Iron Man's death, Dr. Bruce Banner secretly took a small sample from each of the 6 Infinity Stones. Afterwards, he decided with the help of Captain America to return the Infinity Stones to their respective timelines.
Dr. Banner was the only person who knew about this. He conducted hundreds of tests in the Avengers' secret laboratory to build power pills that could give any human an extraordinary ability.
This was the birth of the Power Project.

Composition of the

CAPSULE

Top of the capsule

The top of the capsule is made of edible glass.

It allows the user to see the chemical reaction.

Bottom of the capsule

The underside of the capsule is made primarily of heavy-duty metal. However, it may contain traces of alien materials.

Some users may reject the pill, causing irreversible damage.

Substance inside the capsule

A mixture of liquid and gas resulting from a complex chemical reaction of various terrestrial and extraterrestrial compounds.

The colours of the substance change depending on the compounds used.

Striation in the capsule

The stripes on the underside act as an insulator.

The principle is the same as Thomas Edison's incandescent lamp.

N°01 VELVET

Velvet is one of the five capsules of the Power Project, the remnant of a singularity before the universe.
Velvet is a powerful weapon capable of granting great destructive power.

N°02 TESSERACT

This capsule is used to open portals to other locations and can allow interdimensional travel.
In the case that the transformation fails, the carrier could be projected into a dimension still unknown to this day.

N°03 EMERALD

This capsule could be used to individually change the timeline of individual objects.
Reversing them to a previous state or sending the object to a future state.

N°04 AETHER

The power of the Aether capsule is such that it can affect reality on a universal scale.
Those who fully understand its capabilities can safely absorb it into themselves, as long as they have the courage to resist its effect.

N°05 GEIST

Geist grants whoever holds it total control over the hearts and minds of others.
The capsule contains a very advanced intelligence framework functioning in the same way as a sentient mind.
